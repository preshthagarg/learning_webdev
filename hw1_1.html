Catherine Foundling

Catherine is the main character of the book "A Practical Guide To Evil". She makes the choice to take the knife and cut out the bad parts of the world. Catherine is trying to make the world better but on the side of evil.

Fictional Character Biography

Book 1

Catherine takes the knife, does some shady things. She learns to fight, has some hard lessons, realises her mentor is a monster, but still decides to continue because it will get her into power faster and in a position to help her country. She faces Juniper in war games.

Book 2

Catherine takes out the army. She fights the rebellion fighting to free her home country because she knows they don't have an "after the war" plan and are just fighting for the sake of it.
